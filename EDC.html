Pocket Ready: EDC for Every Adventure

The Nitecore P20iX is a powerful tactical flashlight designed for professional users, including military, law enforcement, and search-and-rescue teams. With a maximum output of 4000 lumens and a beam distance of up to 221 meters, it delivers exceptional visibility even in demanding conditions. Equipped with four CREE XP-L2 V6 LEDs, a STROBE mode for tactical applications, and powered by a 21700i or two CR123 batteries, this flashlight is both versatile and reliable. Its compact dimensions (141.5 mm in length) and lightweight design (116 g) make it ideal for everyday carry and heavy-duty use. With a runtime of up to 350 hours and rechargeable functionality, it offers outstanding convenience and performance.
